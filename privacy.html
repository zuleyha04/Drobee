Privacy Policy (English)

Effective Date: August 10, 2025

At Drobee, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it is stored.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, username (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Device information: Device model, OS version, IP address (may be automatically collected by Firebase).
  • Content data: Uploaded photos, outfit tags, weather tags (stored in Firestore).
  • Location data: City name or coordinates for weather suggestions (manual input or via API).
  • Usage data: In-app navigation and usage statistics (via Google Analytics for Firebase).

2. Purpose of Data Usage

  • Create and manage your account,
  • Store your clothing and outfit records,
  • Provide weather-based outfit suggestions,
  • Analyze and improve app performance,
  • Detect and fix errors.

3. Data Sharing

  • Service providers: Firebase, Google APIs, weather services.
  • Media hosting: Uploaded photos are sent to third-party FreeImageHost API servers for storage and access.
  • Legal compliance: When required by authorities.
  • User consent: With your explicit approval.

4. Data Retention

  • Your account data is permanently deleted when you delete your account.
  • Uploaded images are removed when you delete them.
  • Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request access to, update, or delete your data.
  • Contact: support@drobee.app

6. Contact

For any questions, email us at: support@drobee.app
