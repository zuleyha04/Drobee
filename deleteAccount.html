Drobee - Account Deletion Request

Users can request deletion of their account and associated data by following the steps below:

Account Deletion Steps

  1. Send an email to zuleyhakbas24@gmail.com with the subject “Account Deletion Request”.
  2. Include the email address registered in your Drobee account in your message.
  3. Once your request is received, all account and associated data will be deleted within 7 business days.

Data Deleted / Retained

  • Deleted: Account information, uploaded photos, outfit tags.
  • Retained: Transaction logs required for legal compliance (kept for up to 14 months).

Contact: support@drobee.app
